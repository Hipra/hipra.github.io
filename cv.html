György Bokros Product Designer

I am a multi-disciplinary Product Designer with 12+ years experience in building products for web, mobile and desktop. My front-end skills also helping me to build a bridge between the design, the product team and the development to reach the ideal solution, keeping the business needs in mind. Employment History

Product Designer at Gawker Media

As a team member of a small design team, I work closely with the product and the developer team. Creating rapid and high fidelity prototypes became my daily routine as a type of language for communicating design ideas.

Notable projects:
